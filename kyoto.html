Kyoto, Japan

Exploring Ancient Temples & Traditional Culture

Home Overview Local Tips

City Overview

Kyoto served as Japan's capital and the emperor's residence from 794 until 1868. Known for its numerous classical Buddhist temples, gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines and traditional wooden houses.

Local Tips

Best Time to Visit

Spring (March-May) for cherry blossoms and Fall (October-November) for autumn colors.

Transportation

Get a bus pass for easy access to temples. Rent a bicycle for local exploration.

Cultural Etiquette

Remove shoes when entering temples. Bow when greeting. Maintain quiet in sacred spaces.
